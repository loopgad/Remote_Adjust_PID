Conclusion
----------

The C++ ODE solver with nanobind Python callbacks achieves ~1.3x speedup
over pure-Python RK4. The speedup is limited by the Python callback
dispatch overhead in nanobind. For production use with C++-native ODE
functions, the expected speedup would be > 5x.

All model, orchestrator, and data bus components meet their performance
targets for real-time simulation use.
